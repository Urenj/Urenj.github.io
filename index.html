Artist Info

Hello! I'm AniNeru and I'm an self-taught Digital Artist and Animator and also currently a College student. I also draw in various artstyles.
 
My current artist name "AniNeru" formed because of the inspiration to peoples nickname to Kyoto Animation which is 'KyoAni'. Since I draw Anime, I thought that the japanese translation of my nickname "Neru" and "Ani short for 'Anime /Animation' are good together. But compared to the KyoAni nickname, I did it in vice versa which is now "AniNeru". My previous Artist names are NeruSan and Neruuu
 
I started doing digital arts way back June 2020, I was introduced into 'Pinoy Animation' where I first watched "Asheru Animation" in Facebook Watch. I got entertained by his videos and inspire me to create and learn animation by myself.
 
Since I was a kid, I am fascinated and amazed on Cartoons and Anime especially the cartoons and Anime TV Channels they show so when this opportunity came, it hit my interest. I joined the Internet Art Community in many social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram and lately (the former Twitter) X.
 
Now, I'm still learning to improve my artstyle through watching different artists that teaches fellow artists how to improve their artworks

TERMS & CONDITIONS

• Communication is through facebook messenger or Instagram DMs
• Payment through GCash
• Must pay atleast half or full payment before I draw the Sketch
• Will NOT draw any artstyles that are not listed on my artstyle list
• Please NO TRACING, COPYING and CLAIMING of any of my artworks
• Please DO CREDIT me when you will post or repost my artwork (so much appreciated!)
• Major revisions are limited to minimum of 1-3 only (just before I do the lineart)
• TAT (Turn Around Time) is 1-7 days depending on my schedule
• If my slots are full you'll be in my waiting list (I'll contact you ASAP if I got slots)
• You can make reservations! Just contact me
• Strictly NO RUSHING :'D
• NO REFUNDS
• I have the right to accept or decline your commission if I can't do it
• Any Bad experience will be posted (such as trolling, scamming, etc.) so please be aware

PROCESS OF TRANSACTION

WHAT TO DO?

• Contact my social media accounts if you're interested on commissioning me ^^
• Please READ carefully my TERMS & CONDITIONS before proceeding
• Provide a CLEAR and HD reference photo
• Prices are PER CHARACTER but background and add ons will stay on its said price range
• I'll give you a rough sketch and I'll also ask for further revisions before I continue
• Must pay fully when I start doing the lineart
• Will finish it ASAP (Atleast 2-7 days)
• Frequent updates of the process will be given while working on your commission
• Will give notice to changes on my schedule or any delays that will affect my speed of workflow
• Once finished, I'll send the HD copy through GMAIL or GDRIVE
